Hi, I'm Linnea Cruz!

I'm a Canadian born and raised Graphic Designer, currently residing in Southern California. I'm proficient in Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop and After Effects. I completed a 3 year program of Interactive Media Design at Durham College in Ontario, Canada.

I am currently employed by Dean Myers Chevrolet Buick GMC in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. as a remote Graphic/Web Designer. I've been employed by them since March 2016, but have been working remotely since October 2016.

In my free time I enjoy watercoloring, painting, photography and videography. As an artist, I believe it's important to have several creative outlets.
