Hi, I'm Linnea Cruz!

"A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one."

I'm a Canadian born and raised Graphic Designer, currently residing in Southern California. I'm proficient in Procreate, Animation, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and After Effects. I completed a 3 year program of Interactive Media Design at Durham College in Ontario, Canada back in 2015.

I am currently a Freelance Designer Sub-Contractor with a few years of Social Media Marketing. With my skill in drawing I've developing a distinct art style, and combined my skills that make me the unique designer I am today.

In my free time I enjoy watercoloring, painting, photography and videography.
